Who We Are

The Club

Watch and Learn FC is built on one idea: see it, learn it, own it. We are one club first team, academy, and community combining structured training with real-game understanding so every player grows technically and tactically.

From grassroots to first team, we train on some of the best turf in Kenya. Our academy feeds the club and beyond: scouting opportunities and a clear pathway to top sides are at the heart of what we do.

  • Open to boys and girls,
    ages 4–17
  • Age-specific curriculum
  • Match analysis & video feedback
  • Fitness & conditioning programs
  • Character & leadership development
