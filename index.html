Who We Are

Watch and Learn FC

Watch and Learn FC is built on one idea: see it, learn it, own it. Our club and academy combine structured training with real-game understanding so every player grows technically and tactically.

From grassroots to first team, we’re proud to train on some of the best turf in Kenya. Our academy has produced players who have joined top clubs scouting opportunities and a clear pathway are at the heart of what we do.

  • ✓ Age-specific curriculum
  • ✓ Match analysis & video feedback
  • ✓ Fitness & conditioning programs
  • ✓ Character & leadership development
